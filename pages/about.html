About SILVOL

Our mission is simple: empower people through learning and opportunity.

SILVOL wants to close the digital divide and help people of all ages and backgrounds build the skills and confidence to thrive in a digital world.

Our Story

SILVOL was created in response to a growing need for accessible, practical digital training in communities across the UK. We bring together education, technology and human support to deliver life-changing impact.
